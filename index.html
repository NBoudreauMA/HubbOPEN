🏛 Town Administrator’s Message ▼

Welcome to **Hubbardston’s Budget Portal**, where financial transparency meets innovation. Our **2026 fiscal plan** reflects a balance between **responsibility, investment, and long-term growth**. We remain committed to **data-driven decision-making, resident engagement, and financial sustainability**.

💰 Revenue Overview ▼

Hubbardston's **financial backbone** is supported by three primary revenue streams: **🔹 Property Tax (Tax Levy)** - The core funding source for essential services. **🔹 State Aid** - Grants & allocations for education and infrastructure. **🔹 Local Receipts** - Fees, permits, and other town-generated income. These sources ensure the town remains financially stable while continuing to grow.
