Dear Residents of Hubbardston,

I am pleased to present the Fiscal Year 2026 budget proposal for the Town of Hubbardston. This budget reflects our ongoing commitment to fiscal responsibility while investing in critical services and infrastructure that our community needs.

Downtown Hubbardston - A Community Moving Forward

Budget Highlights

The FY26 budget totals $11.6 million, representing a 4.8% increase from FY25. Key priorities include:

  • Public Safety Enhancement: Funding for additional police officer and fire equipment
  • Infrastructure Investment: Road maintenance and facility improvements
  • Education Excellence: Supporting our school district's strategic initiatives
  • Environmental Stewardship: Investment in open space preservation

Tax Impact

Average tax increase of $185 per household

State Aid

2.7% increase in state funding

Free Cash

$1.2M certified free cash

Looking ahead, we face both challenges and opportunities. Rising costs in healthcare and utilities require careful financial planning. However, our strong financial position and growing commercial base provide stability for future investments.

Sincerely,

Nathan Boudreau, MPA, MCPPO
Town Administrator
