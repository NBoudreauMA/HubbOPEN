Hubbardston's revenue sources include Tax Levy, State Aid, and Local Receipts.

Major Revenue Categories

Revenue Source FY23 FY24 FY25 FY26 Explanation
Tax Levy ⓘ $8,052,778 $8,425,343 $8,852,568 $9,276,259
The **Tax Levy** is the largest source of revenue and comes from property taxes. It increases annually due to Proposition 2.5, which limits tax increases but allows for new growth adjustments.
State Aid ⓘ $668,674 $682,452 $711,871 $730,906
**State Aid** consists of government funding to support education, public safety, and general government functions. The largest portion is **Unrestricted General Government Aid (UGGA)**.
Local Receipts ⓘ $1,364,392 $1,478,687 $1,502,964 $1,619,684
**Local Receipts** include motor vehicle excise taxes, permit fees, fines, and investment income. This revenue fluctuates based on economic conditions and policy changes.

Expenditures include public safety, education, public works, and general government services.

The Capital Improvement Plan (CIP) outlines long-term investments in town infrastructure and facilities.
