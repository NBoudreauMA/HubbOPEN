Hubbardston's revenue is primarily generated from three major sources: **Tax Levy, State Aid, and Local Receipts**. The **Tax Levy** makes up the majority, followed by **State Aid**, which provides funding for essential services, and **Local Receipts**, which include excise taxes, permits, and fees.
