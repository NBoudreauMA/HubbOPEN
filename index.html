Key Investments & Initiatives

Hubbardston is making strategic investments in infrastructure, public safety, and technology to improve efficiency and service delivery.

  • Infrastructure: Roadway improvements and municipal building upgrades.
  • Public Safety: Enhanced funding for police and fire services.
  • Technology: Digital transformation to streamline operations and engage residents.
